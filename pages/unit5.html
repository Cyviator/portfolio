Unit 5: An Introduction to Security and Risk Standards in Industry and the Enterprise

Wiki Activity - Security Frameworks

This artefact presents my contribution to the Unit 5 Wiki Activity, where I assessed which security and risk management frameworks (e.g., ISO/IEC 27001, ISO 31000, COBIT, and NIST CSF) would best suit an international bank, a large hospital, and a food manufacturing factory. The process of comparing sector-specific requirements with the scope and strengths of each framework deepened my understanding of how compliance, governance, and operational context interrelate across different industries.
