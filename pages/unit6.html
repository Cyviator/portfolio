Unit 6: The Practical Implications of Security and Risk Standards

Seminar 4 – Security Standards Discussion

This artefact presents my contribution to Seminar 4, where I applied key security standards, including GDPR and PCI DSS, to the Pampered Pets case study. I analysed the company's regulatory obligations, proposed compliance audits using ISO/IEC 27001 and PCI DSS self-assessment questionnaires, and highlighted the risks associated with third-party payment providers. The tutor commended my analysis as pragmatic and well-applied, particularly my emphasis on third-party risks. My peer also agreed with my recommendations, noting their alignment with his own findings. The discussion reinforced my understanding of how contextual factors shape the practical implementation of security and data protection standards.

We identified and prioritised risks and presented recommendations based on the combined risk profiles and business objectives. The project provided valuable experience in managing collaborative challenges while maintaining academic and professional standards under pressure. It further strengthened my ability to stay organised, communicate effectively, and ensure successful project completion even when collaboration required additional coordination and effort.
