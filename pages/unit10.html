Unit 10: Practical Applications and Issues in DR Implementations

Unit 10 Seminar Preparation - Workshop Activity

This artefact presents my summary and comparative analysis of Sutton (2021) and Popov, Lyon and Hollcroft (2022). As there was no separate workshop activity in this unit, I created this summary to consolidate my understanding of disaster recovery concepts such as RPO, RTO, DRaaS, and the challenges of vendor lock-in, resilience, and network security. The exercise helped me connect theoretical principles with practical design considerations for disaster recovery strategies. I completed this work to support my preparation for the individual executive summary in Unit 11, where disaster recovery requirements formed part of the graded assessment.
